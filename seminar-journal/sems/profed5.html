ConnectEd: Harnessing Technology in Teaching and Learning

Insight no. 5

Attending the PROFED1 seminar on "The Child and Adolescent Learners and Learning Principles" was a transformative experience, shedding light on essential aspects crucial for my future as an educator. Mr. Francis E. Factura, our speaker, delivered a comprehensive overview of key topics, enriching my understanding of effective teaching practices.

The nature of a child and the philosophies influencing the teaching profession emphasized the foundational principles shaping education. Understanding the seven principles of learning underscored the importance of diverse teaching methods for individualized learning. Exploring the four major developmental theories offered a holistic perspective on child and adolescent development, informing adaptable teaching approaches. Additionally, the session on RA7610 and the DepEd Child Protection Policy highlighted the need for creating a safe and nurturing learning environment.

This seminar has not only deepened my understanding of the principles underlying child and adolescent learning but has also motivated me to approach my future role as an educator with a more informed and compassionate mindset. It provided a rich tapestry of insights, encompassing effective learning principles, philosophies guiding teaching, diverse instructional strategies, and the paramount importance of safeguarding students' well-being.

These comprehensive learnings are poised to significantly impact my future practices as an aspiring educators, contributing to the creation of more informed, adaptable, and compassionate teaching environments.
